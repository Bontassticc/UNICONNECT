About UniConnect

UniConnect is your central hub for all university events — academic, social, and sports. It helps students discover new opportunities, connect with peers, and never miss what’s happening on campus.

Our Mission

We aim to make student life more connected by simplifying event discovery and participation. Whether you’re hosting a workshop, attending a social mixer, or joining a sports event — UniConnect helps you stay in the loop.
